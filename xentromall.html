Description

XentroMall Antipolo, located along Sumulong Highway in Barangay Mambugan, Antipolo City, officially opened its doors to the public on June 21, 2018. Developed and managed by XRC Mall Developer, Inc., the mall was built to provide a convenient shopping destination for the growing community in Antipolo and nearby areas, offering a more accessible alternative to larger malls in Metro Manila. Designed as a three-story community mall, it houses a variety of retail shops, dining establishments, and service centers, aiming to meet the everyday needs of local residents. The mall gained national recognition in 2018 when it won the Golden Kubeta Award for maintaining one of the cleanest public restrooms in the country. In a significant development, XentroMall Antipolo was declared a special economic zone on November 22, 2024, under Proclamation No. 752, designating it as an Information Technology (IT) center. This ecozone status allows for business incentives and encourages IT-related enterprises to operate within its premises. With a land area of 10,898 square meters and a floor area of over 27,000 square meters, the mall continues to play an essential role in both the local economy and community life in Antipolo.

XentroMall Antipolo stands out among community malls for several notable reasons. Most significantly, it was declared a special economic zone on November 22, 2024, under Proclamation No. 752, officially designating it as an Information Technology (IT) Center. This means the mall is now recognized as a hub for IT and BPO companies, making it eligible for government incentives through the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA). The ecozone status not only boosts business opportunities in the area but also positions the mall as a potential employment center in Antipolo. Additionally, XentroMall Antipolo has become a key part of the local community due to its accessible location along Sumulong Highway in Barangay Mambugan, offering a convenient destination for shopping, dining, and services.
